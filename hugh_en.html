Hugh

Hugh is a seasoned warrior of Stonegard, respected for his strength, endurance, and battlefield experience. Years of combat have shaped him into a reliable and formidable protector. He is known for his straightforward nature and strong sense of loyalty. Hugh believes that actions speak louder than words and that courage is proven through deeds. Despite his tough exterior, he deeply cares for his comrades and will stand by them even in the darkest moments.
