Brienne

Brienne is a determined and resilient warrior known for her courage and unwavering sense of justice. She never backs down from a challenge and stands firm even in the face of overwhelming odds. Her strength lies not only in her combat skills but also in her loyalty and strong moral compass. She believes in protecting the innocent and fighting for what is right, no matter the cost. Though she may appear stern and unyielding, Brienne possesses a deeply compassionate heart and a quiet dedication to those she serves.
