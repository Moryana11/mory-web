Zarveth

Zarveth is a towering and fearsome warlord whose presence alone can silence a battlefield. His immense strength and relentless determination make him one of the most dangerous enemies to face. He leads with dominance and discipline, commanding both fear and respect from those who serve under him. To his followers, he is a symbol of power and unbreakable will. Behind his brutal reputation lies a mind driven by purpose and an unwavering belief in victory, no matter the cost.
