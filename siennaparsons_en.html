Sienna Parsons

Sienna Parsons is a respected mage and leader known for her wisdom, composure, and unwavering self-control. She carries herself with dignity and authority, inspiring confidence in those who follow her guidance. Her strength lies not only in her magical abilities but also in her sharp mind and ability to remain calm in the most difficult situations. She believes that knowledge and discipline are the true foundations of power. Though she often appears strict and reserved, Parsons is deeply devoted to protecting others and maintaining balance in a world threatened by chaos and darkness.
