Rowena

Rowena comes from Kastleton, a bustling port city in the land of Laslan, where she grew up surrounded by people from many different cultures. At a young age, she was chosen to train in the Purelight Tower, becoming a healer with deep knowledge of ancient texts and sacred rituals. Her nature is introspective, quiet, and caring; she often places the needs of others before her own.
