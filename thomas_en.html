Thomas

Thomas, a knight of Stonegard Castle in the land of Stonegard, was raised within a strict military order where honor, discipline, and strength meant everything. He is an experienced warrior who wields a sword and spear and is capable of leading men in the field. He is honorable, proud, stubborn, and quick in his decisions; yet he is deeply loyal and determined to protect the innocent, even at the cost of his own life.
