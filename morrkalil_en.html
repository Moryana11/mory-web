Morr’kalil

Morr’kalil is a dark God of blood, suffering, and death, dwelling within the Darkness.

He is obsessed with suffering and desires to reshape the world into an endless temple of pain. His followers serve him in fanatical faith, believing that through pain comes purification.

He wields ancient dark magic that allows him to drain life force from living beings, control blood and souls, and raise the dead. He can spread a plague of darkness, transform people into twisted servants, and open rifts between the world of the living and the realm of death.

His presence breaks the will and awakens fear and madness in the hearts of mortals.
