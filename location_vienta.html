Vienta

Vienta is an exotic city located in the southwest of the land of Stonegrad, on the continent of Solisium. On its western side, it is washed by the sea, while to the east stretches the endless desert known as the Monolithic Wasteland — a realm of scorching sun, sand, and ancient secrets. Thanks to its location, Vienta is a place where the power of the ocean meets the relentless force of the desert. The city is renowned for its fire mages, who have lived here for generations. Children gifted with magical talent are raised and trained from an early age to master both the destructive and creative power of fire. Here, fire is not only a weapon, but also a symbol of faith, honor, and life energy. In the streets of Vienta, it is common to see flames dancing in the hands of apprentices and seasoned masters alike. The dominant landmark of the city is the majestic temple known as the Sanctuary of the Crimson Breath, the largest sanctuary not only in Vienta but in all of Stonegrad. It is regarded as the sacred center of fire magic and the spiritual heart of the land. Pilgrims, mages, and rulers come to its richly adorned halls seeking strength, blessings, or answers to questions that cannot be found anywhere else. Vienta is a city of heat, faith, and discipline. A city where the power of fire was born, and where every flame can be a light of hope… or the beginning of destruction.
