Turayne Ruins

Turayne was once a prosperous city located along the Grassland Trail — an ancient trade route that wound between mountains and dense forests. For many years, it was the most important trading hub in this part of the land of Laslan, long before the port city of Kastleton assumed that role. Merchants, pilgrims, and messengers passed through its gates, and the city flourished thanks to commerce and the protection of powerful mages. For centuries, Turayne was guarded by a lineage of strong mages who devoted their lives to its safety and to maintaining balance in the region. The people of the city lived with the certainty that their protectors could repel any danger. But on the day of the funeral of the last High Mage, everything changed. On that very day, the Shadowmages of the Sylvean Order launched their attack. A war broke out — brutal and swift — in which the remaining mages of the city fought in vain against the invaders. Turayne fell, and all that remained of the city were ruins.
