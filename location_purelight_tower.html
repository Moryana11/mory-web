Purelight Tower

Purelight Tower stands to the east, not far from the ruins of the ancient city of Turayne in the land of Laslan, on the continent of Solisium. The tower rises atop a gentle hill surrounded by peaceful fields and quiet forests, shining like a beacon of hope in a land scarred by tragedy and darkness. Built from smooth white stone, the tower is known throughout Laslan as the center of healing magic and the sanctuary of healer mages. For generations, gifted individuals have traveled here to study the arts of restoration, purification, and spiritual balance. Young healers are trained under the guidance of experienced masters who dedicate their lives to preserving knowledge and protecting the fragile balance between life and death. Within the tower are vast healing halls, libraries filled with ancient scrolls, meditation chambers, and sacred sanctuaries where wounded souls and bodies may recover in peace. At the heart of the tower burns the Eternal Flame of Pure Light, a sacred fire said to symbolize hope, life, and purity. Legends claim the flame has never extinguished, not even during the darkest eras of war and despair. Despite standing close to the cursed Ruins of Turayne, Purelight Tower remains untouched by corruption. Many believe powerful protective magic surrounds the sanctuary, shielding it from the darkness spreading across the land. The healers who dwell within rarely involve themselves in politics or war, yet they are respected throughout Solisium for their wisdom, compassion, and unwavering devotion to preserving life.
