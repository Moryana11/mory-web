Purelight tower

The Purelight tower stands to the east, just a short distance from the ruins of the city of Turayne in the land of Laslan, on the continent of Solisium. It rises on a gentle hill surrounded by a peaceful landscape, like a beacon of hope in a land marked by ancient tragedy. This tall, slender tower of white stone is regarded as the center of all healer mages in the entire land of Laslan. For generations, people gifted with healing magic have come here to learn how to master their abilities and serve others. Healers are trained here from a young age under the guidance of experienced masters who pass their knowledge on to future generations. Inside the tower are healing halls, study rooms filled with ancient scrolls, and quiet chambers intended for meditation and focus. At its heart burns the Eternal Flame of Pure Light — a symbol of life, healing, and hope. It is said that its radiance has never faded, even in the darkest of times. Despite the proximity of the cursed Ruins of Turayne, the Purelight tower remains a place of peace and safety. The healers who dwell within watch over the balance between life and death and stand ready to help anyone who crosses its threshold.
