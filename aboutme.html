About Me

Writing stories is something that truly brings me joy and fulfillment. It is a world I can dive into whenever I need to relax or organize my thoughts and emotions.

My love for fantasy and the medieval world was inspired by my parents, who always supported this passion. Thanks to them, I developed a strong connection to stories, adventures, and worlds where almost anything is possible.
