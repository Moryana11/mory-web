About me

Writing stories is something that truly brings me joy and fulfillment. It is a world I can immerse myself in whenever I need to rest, escape from reality, or sort through my own thoughts and emotions. My love for fantasy and the medieval world was inspired by my family, who always supported me in this passion. Because of them, I developed a deep connection to stories, adventures, and worlds where almost anything is possible.
