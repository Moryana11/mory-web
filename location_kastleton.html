Kastleton

Kastleton is a bustling port city located on the western coast of the land of Laslan, on the continent of Solisium. From early morning until deep into the night, the city is alive with the noise of arriving ships, the shouting of sailors, and the scent of spices, salt, fish, and distant lands. Merchants from across the world gather in its harbor — from wealthy traders carrying silk and precious stones to exhausted travelers searching for opportunity, refuge, or a new beginning. Its narrow stone streets are filled with lively markets, crowded taverns, and noisy inns where dozens of languages and stories intertwine. By day, Kastleton thrives on trade and diplomacy; by night, it becomes a city of secrets, whispers, and shadows. Despite its chaotic appearance, strict order and pride shape the lives of its people. The city is ruled by Lord Hobs Kastler, a respected noble known for his discipline, firm leadership, and unwavering authority. Under his rule, Kastleton has become one of the most important trade centers in Laslan and a secure refuge for those fleeing war and uncertainty. Yet Kastleton's importance reaches far beyond commerce. The headquarters of the Allied Resistance Forces of Laslan are hidden within the city, where strategies, intelligence, and secret operations against the enemies of Solisium are coordinated. Because of this, Kastleton stands not only as a center of wealth and trade, but also as one of the greatest symbols of resistance and hope on the continent. Kastleton is a city of contrasts — wealth and poverty, loyalty and betrayal, ambition and fear. It is a place where destinies cross, alliances are forged, and where every newcomer may either discover a new future… or lose everything.
