Jacque Hander

Jacque Hander is a battle-hardened commander of Stonegard, feared by enemies and respected by his soldiers. His powerful presence and relentless fighting style have earned him a reputation as one of the most formidable warriors in the kingdom. He leads from the front, never asking his men to face dangers he would not confront himself. Strength, discipline, and loyalty are the foundations of his command. Though his temper can be fierce and his methods uncompromising, Hander remains deeply devoted to protecting Stonegard and ensuring the survival of his people in times of war.
