Aaliyah

Aaliyah comes from the port city of Vienta on the edge of the Monolithic Desert in the land of Stonegrad. She is a powerful fire mage who underwent training at the Sanctuary of the Red Breath, to control the destructive and life-giving force of flame. Her magic is fierce, precise, and driven by strong emotions. Her personality is bold, confident, and passionate. She faces danger without hesitation and often hides her care for others behind humor and a fearless smile.
