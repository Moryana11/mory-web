Rowena

Rowena comes from Kastleton, a bustling port city in the land of Laslan, where from an early age she grew up among people of many different cultures.

As a child, she was chosen to train at the Tower of Pure Light, which shaped her into a healer with deep knowledge of ancient texts and rituals.

Her nature is rather introspective, quiet, and caring; she often places others before herself.
